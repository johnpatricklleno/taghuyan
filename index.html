About Us

Welcome To Taghuyan

Taghuyan is a two-hectare farm lot atop a ravine, overlooking a stream. The area is lined with pine trees, a lush garden of local flora, and a pond

This venue is a good respite for anyone to experience simple farm life. It is open for special events and occasions.

Bahay Bulakan

A farm house that accommodates 8-10 pax minimum. The house has a kitchen, bathroom, a living room area, a master bedroom, and extra beds.

Alimyon Cafe

Alimyon offers homemade baked goods, beverages, and seasonal products fresh from our gardens such as jams, herbal vinegars, and many more.

Events
